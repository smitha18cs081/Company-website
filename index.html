What we do
Managed Data Services: Entrust all of your data domains to a cloud-based service that evolves with the market. Built on a lean data approach, RIMES solves for the pitfalls of legacy operating models. Unlock a new level in quality, efficiency, and adaptability across the full universe of financial data.
RegTech Solutions: We are ideally positioned to offer a strategic solution to Benchmarks regulation compliance. The RIMES RegFocus℠ solutions build on the proven RIMES technology but address these specific BMR challenges.
Benchmark Data Service: RIMES BDS® is a fully managed data service which enables investment managers, owners, and pension funds to maximize operational efficiency and scalability, improve data quality and minimize the internal costs of data management while maintaining full control.
ETF Data Management: ■ We source composition data directly from ETF issuers and provide the full complement of composition files, including holdings and all constituent baskets ■ Leveraging our market-leading data partner ecosystem and managed service, we have the ability to cross-reference and enrich with 3rd party content to meet your specifications
